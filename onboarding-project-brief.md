## Client Overview

Summit Dental Care is a family and cosmetic dental practice in Galway City, established in 2012. They serve approximately 3,000 active patients with a team of 12 staff including three dentists, two hygienists, and a practice manager. The practice recently expanded into an adjacent unit to add a third treatment room.

Their current website (summitdentalcare.ie) was built in 2018 on a default WordPress theme and has received minimal updates since. Key issues include broken mobile layouts, 6.2s page load times, no online booking integration, and over 2 years of content silence. The practice positions itself as a premium provider and is actively looking to grow its cosmetic dentistry segment among 25–40 year olds.

The decision maker is Eileen Fogarty, Practice Manager. She was identified via the Google Business profile and confirmed as the primary contact during the discovery call.

## Project Goals

- Replace the dated WordPress site with a modern, mobile-first design that reflects the practice's premium positioning
- Integrate online booking so new patients can book appointments directly from the website
- Improve page load performance to under 3 seconds on mobile
- Establish a content foundation for ongoing SEO and patient engagement (blog, treatment pages)
- Display Google Business reviews on the homepage to build trust with prospective patients
- Create a scalable page structure that can grow as the practice adds new services

## Scope Summary

**Included:**
- 7-page responsive website (Home, Treatments, Invisalign & Cosmetics, About, Book Online, Contact)
- WordPress rebuild with lightweight theme (GeneratePress) and custom child theme
- Online booking integration (pending confirmation of booking system)
- SEO foundation: meta data, Schema.org markup, sitemap, GA4 setup
- Content migration and navigation restructure (14 flat items → logical hierarchy)
- CMS training for the practice team
- Image optimisation (WebP with fallbacks)

**Excluded:**
- Professional photography (recommended — practice to arrange separately)
- Copywriting for treatment pages (practice to provide clinical descriptions)
- Social media management
- Ongoing hosting/maintenance (available as separate retainer)
- Paid advertising (Google Ads, social)
