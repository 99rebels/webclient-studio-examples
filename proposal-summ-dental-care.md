## Executive Summary

Summit Dental Care has built a strong reputation in Galway over the past 14 years, with a loyal patient base and growing cosmetic dentistry segment. However, the current website — built in 2018 on a default WordPress theme — no longer reflects the quality of care the practice provides. Mobile users (who account for an estimated 65%+ of healthcare searches) encounter broken layouts and slow load times. There is no way to book an appointment online, despite this being the primary action new patients want to take.

This proposal covers a complete redesign and rebuild of summitdentalcare.ie: a modern, mobile-first website that converts visitors into booked appointments, integrates with the practice's booking workflow, and establishes a content foundation for ongoing SEO and patient engagement.

## Scope of Work

### What's Included

**Design & Development**
- Custom responsive website designed mobile-first, tested on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and desktop
- Modern, clean aesthetic that reflects the practice's premium positioning
- Built on WordPress with a lightweight, well-supported theme (e.g., GeneratePress or Astra) and a custom child theme
- Page builder-free for performance — all layouts built with clean HTML/CSS and a minimal block pattern approach
- Integration with the practice's preferred online booking system ([Confirm with client: which booking system do they use? Do they want to switch?])

**Key Pages**
| Page | Purpose | Key Features |
|---|---|---|
| Home | First impression, trust signals, primary CTA | Hero with CTA to book, review highlights, treatment categories, team preview |
| Treatments | Service catalogue | Individual pages for General, Cosmetic, Emergency dentistry with before/after gallery structure |
| Invisalign & Cosmetics | Premium service showcase | Dedicated landing page for high-value cosmetic procedures |
| About the Practice | Trust and credibility | Team profiles with photos, practice history, certifications, technology |
| Book Online | Primary conversion goal | Integrated booking — the most important page on the site |
| Contact & Location | Practical information | Map embed, opening hours, phone (tap-to-call on mobile), parking info |

**Technical Deliverables**
- Page load time under 3 seconds on mobile (Lighthouse, 4G simulation)
- SSL certificate active on all pages with no mixed content
- SEO-optimised page structure: unique meta titles and descriptions on every page, Schema.org markup for local business and medical practice
- Google Business profile integration: reviews displayed on the homepage, Q&A linked
- CMS training: the practice team can update blog posts, team bios, and treatment descriptions without developer help
- Image optimisation: all existing and new images compressed and served in WebP format with fallbacks
- Google Analytics 4 and Search Console setup

**Content Migration**
- Migrate all existing page content to the new site
- Restructure the navigation from 14 flat items to a logical 2-level hierarchy
- Rewrite the homepage and "About" page copy for clarity and conversion
- [Confirm with client: do they have professional team photos and treatment images, or should we budget for a photography session?]

### What's NOT Included
- Professional photography (recommend arranging separately — critical for a dental practice site)
- Copywriting for all treatment pages (the practice should provide accurate clinical descriptions)
- Social media management or content creation (can be discussed as a separate retainer)
- Ongoing website hosting, maintenance, or security monitoring (available as a monthly retainer — see below)
- Online booking system licence fees (third-party cost, typically €30–80/month)
- Google Ads or paid search campaigns
- Email marketing setup or automation

### Assumptions
- The practice will provide final content (team bios, treatment descriptions, photos) within 2 weeks of project kick-off
- The existing domain and hosting will be used (no domain migration required)
- The practice's booking system has an embeddable widget or API available
- No custom backend development or patient portal integration is required
